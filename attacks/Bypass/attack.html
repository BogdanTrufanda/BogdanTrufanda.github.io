Advantages implementing Whitelists & CAPTCHAs

CAPTCHAs have several applications for practical security, including (but not limited to):

  • Preventing Comment Spam in Blogs.
  • Protecting Website Registration
  • Protecting Email Addresses From Scrapers..
  • Online Polls.
  • Preventing Dictionary Attacks.
  • Preventing Worms and Spam.

Here are the benefits of using an application whitelisting:

  • You can spot right away any unknown program running in your network or system. Looking at the whitelist you can say when a program is a threat or not. If it is not listed then, it is not allowed.
